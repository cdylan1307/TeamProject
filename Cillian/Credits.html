Credits

The Legendary Team Behind the Game

🏆 Team 2 🏆

Created with passion, dedication, and countless hours of pixel art, programming, and game design. This project represents our journey through Greek mythology and game development.

🎨

Cillian Lynch

Lead Designer, Web Developer & Programmer

Hand-drew and animated all Achilles and Patroclus character sprites with over 70+ frames. Programmed character movement systems and integrated all animations into the game engine. Built this complete website from scratch to showcase the team's work.

⚔️

Alan Haugh

Environment Designer & Systems Developer

Designed enemy characters, backgrounds, and environmental assets. Developed the dealer/shop system and menu functionality. Implemented the Bard character with music playback system and created the arena atmosphere.

🎮

Dylan Cooney

Lead Programmer & Level Designer

Architected the three-level structure and game progression system. Designed the companion mechanics and boss fight sequences. Wrote the game's narrative structure and documentation.

👾

Jihan Xu

Enemy Designer & Combat Systems

Created enemy AI and combat behaviors. Designed and implemented various enemy types including archers, soldiers, and the Hector boss. Developed menu systems and game transitions.
