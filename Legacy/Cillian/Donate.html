Why Donate?

This game represents hundreds of hours of work from Team 2. Over 100 animation frames hand-drawn pixel by pixel, thousands of lines of Python code written and debugged, complex character movement systems, AI behaviors, and game mechanics all crafted with dedication.

Your donation helps support:

  • 💻 Future game development and programming projects
  • 🎨 More hand-drawn pixel art, animations, and designs
  • 📚 Learning resources, courses, and tools for the team
  • ☕ Coffee for those late-night coding and drawing sessions
  • 🎮 Game development software, IDEs, and art tools
  • ⚙️ Better hardware for faster development

Note: This is a student project built from scratch. Every contribution, no matter how small, means the world to us and motivates us to keep creating games!
