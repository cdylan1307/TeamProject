The Legend Unfolds

Achilles and Patroclus is a top-down 2D action game built in Python using Pygame. Journey through three immersive levels inspired by the mythological story of Achilles and Patroclus, spanning different parts of the ancient Greek narrative.

Control the legendary warrior Achilles with Patroclus as your faithful companion in intense arena-based combat. Face waves of enemies and powerful bosses as you progress through this mythological adventure.

The story culminates in an emotional final level where Patroclus is removed from gameplay, leading to an epic final confrontation with Hector, Prince of Troy.
